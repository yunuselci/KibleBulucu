# Qibla Finder Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2025

## Introduction

Thank you for using the Qibla Finder application. This privacy policy explains how your personal data is collected, used, and protected when you use our application.

## Data Collection

### Location Information
- The app uses your device's location information to determine the Qibla direction
- Location information is only used to calculate the Qibla direction
- Location information is processed locally on your device and is not sent to any server

## Data Usage

The collected data is used for the following purposes:
- Accurate calculation of Qibla direction
- Providing the basic functionality of the application

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device
- Your data is not shared with third parties
- No data is sent to any server

## User Rights

As users, you have the following rights:
- Change location permissions at any time
- Delete all your data by uninstalling the application
